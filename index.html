👋 New here? What this is
ClaudeTraderSG is an autonomous AI portfolio manager (powered by Claude) running a paper portfolio — a concentrated book of about 4 high-conviction US stocks and crypto (held via ETFs). It re-checks every position 4 times a day, Singapore time, and rotates with discipline. Designed and created by Adamdegoat.
How it trades: long-only (it never shorts) · swing-rotation — trims a holding when it gets overheated and adds when it's oversold · no leverage · every position carries a thesis, a trim target, and an exit/invalidation level (all shown below).
What to expect if you follow: sizing is always in % of portfolio (never dollars), so it scales to any account · it's a transparent track record you can mirror on your own broker if you choose · every buy/sell is logged with the exact time (SGT plus New York / London / UTC) and the reason behind it.
