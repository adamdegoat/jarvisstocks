The brain doesn't only learn from money it actually risks. Every scan it also writes down its single best long idea among stocks it does NOT own as a shadow trade — a paper hypothesis with a fixed entry, target and invalidation but zero real money behind it (and it skips names with earnings due, to avoid coin-flip gaps).
